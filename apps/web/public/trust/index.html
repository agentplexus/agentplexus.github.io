Our Security Commitments

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Control: Strict authentication and authorization for all systems
  • Minimal Data Collection: We collect only what's necessary to provide our services
  • No Data Sales: We never sell your personal information or communication content
  • Secure Development: We follow security best practices including code reviews and vulnerability scanning
  • Incident Response: We have procedures to detect, respond to, and notify about security incidents

Compliance

Our services are designed with compliance in mind:

  • TCPA: Our messaging services support opt-out handling and consent management
  • CTIA Guidelines: We follow carrier best practices for SMS/MMS messaging
  • A2P 10DLC: Registered for application-to-person messaging in the US
  • GDPR Ready: Data subject rights supported for EU users
  • CCPA Ready: California privacy rights supported
